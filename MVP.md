### The Social Chef

# Description
This application is for people who want to share recipes in a social setting. 

## MVP
# What can users do?
- share recipes 
- rate the recipes
    - include comment
- search for recipes
    - search by name
    - search by ingredient
- share a bio page 
    - has list of contributed recipes
    - personal info
    - picture
-

- user
- recipe

- comment
